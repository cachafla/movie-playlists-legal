Movie Playlists

AI-curated movie lists to create and share with friends

Describe the movies you want in plain English and let AI build the perfect playlist. Track what you watch, explore with friends, and never run out of ideas.

Features

  • AI playlists — Type "cozy 90s comedies" or "mind-bending sci-fi" and get a curated lineup in seconds
  • Want to Watch & Watched — Keep track of what you plan to see and what you've already seen, with stats and filters
  • Friends & sharing — Add friends, share playlists, see what they've watched, and get recommendations
  • Explore — Discover trending and top-rated movies, filter by genre, decade, language, and more
  • Where to Watch — Find where to stream, rent, or buy any movie
  • Trailers & ratings — Watch trailers in-app and see IMDb, Rotten Tomatoes, and Metacritic ratings at a glance
