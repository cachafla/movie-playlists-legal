Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a playlist?

Tap the + button on the Playlists tab. You can describe what you're looking for in plain English (e.g., "cozy 90s comedies") and let AI generate a lineup, or search for and add movies manually.

How do AI recommendations work?

Type a description of the kind of movies you want — genre, era, mood, theme, or anything else — and the App uses AI to build a curated playlist. Each movie is scored and filtered to match your request as closely as possible.

How do I add friends?

Go to the Friends tab and tap Add Friend. Search by name or email. Your friend will receive a friend request notification and can accept or decline.

How do I share a playlist?

Open any playlist, tap the share icon, and choose a friend. You can grant view-only or edit access. They'll receive a notification with a link to the playlist.

How do I mark a movie as watched?

Open the movie's detail page and tap Mark as Watched. You can also mark movies from within a playlist. Watched movies appear in your stats and history.

Where do the ratings come from?

Movie Playlists shows ratings from IMDb, Rotten Tomatoes, and Metacritic aggregated in one view so you don't have to check multiple apps.

How do I sign in with Apple instead of Google?

On the sign-in screen, tap Sign in with Apple. If you already have an account with the same email address, it will link automatically — your playlists and data carry over seamlessly.

How do I delete my account?

Go to Profile → Delete Account. Deletion is permanent and removes all your playlists, watch history, and friend connections from our servers.

The app is crashing or behaving unexpectedly

Make sure you're running the latest version of the App and the latest version of iOS. If the problem persists, email us with your device model, iOS version, and a description of what happened and we'll look into it.

How do I turn off push notifications?

Go to Settings → Notifications → Movie Playlists on your device and toggle notifications off. You can also control individual notification types there.
